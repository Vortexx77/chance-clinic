Our Story

Founded in 2019, Chanse Physician & Children's Clinic began with a vision to provide accessible, affordable and high-quality healthcare to our community. What started as a small medical facility has grown into a big specialised clinic serving thousands of patients annually.

Today, we continue to expand our services and facilities while maintaining our commitment to excellence in patient care, integrity, and research.
